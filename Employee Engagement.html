TCS Maitree



    TCS - Maitree is a platform for associates to meet, interact and contribute. It assists in bonding with the associates within the firm across the globe. It represents the face of “Associate Volunteering” in the field of Employee Engagement and Community Initiatives in TCS. TCS - Maitree (the word means friendship) was started in February 2002, with an aim to deploy Corporate Sustainability through volunteering for the internal Community (TCSers and their family members), external community (underprivileged /differently-abled communities), and the environment.

    1. Calendar Events

    Spelling Bee, Article Writing Competition

    2. Associate Driven Clubs

    Poetry Club, Toastmasters Club, Table Tennis Workshops, etc

    3. Special Days

    4. External Sports

    5. Education and Skill Development

    6. Health Awareness

    7. Environmental Sustainability

    8. Others/Charity


TCS Fit4Life



    TCS Fit4Life, is an initiative that concept marries wellness, team spirit, and social cause into one. This unique workplace-organized fitness initiative is designed to inspire fun, fitness, and camaraderie in competition and, of course, for the social good. Some of the initiatives under this are:

    Yoga, Aerobics sessions - Twice/Thrice a week- By trained External Instructors

    Stretching sessions - Once/Twice a week

    Gymnastics, Marathon, Running, Brisk Walking, Zumba, and other physical activities
