Advances

There are 3 types of Advances



    I. Salary

    For New Joiners:

    If the first month's salary is yet to be processed, he/she is entitled to claim 2 months of a basic salary advance.

    For Existing Employees:

    He/she can claim 1 month of basic salary as advance.

    Criteria for Claim:

    An employee can claim advances 3 times in an FY. However, they should have repaid the previous advance before claiming it again.

    II. Sundry

    This is a form of financial assistance that the employees can claim in case of they attend training, outings and perform other miscellaneous activities all related to the official purpose only.

    Criteria for Claim:

    An employee is eligible for Rs. 50,000/FY and can be claimed ‘n’ times/FY. However, they should have repaid the previous advance before claiming it again. If an employee wants to claim a certain amount under Sundry, he/she must do it within 60 days of incurring the expenses by providing all the supporting documents.

    III. Travel

    This is a form of financial assistance that the employees can claim for bearing travel expenses, food and accommodation while serving an official purpose.

    Criteria for Claim:

    An employee can claim from Rs. 1000/day to Rs. 10,000/ travel, ‘n’ times an FY. However, they should have repaid the previous advance before claiming it again. If an employee wants to claim a certain amount under Travel, he/she must do it within 30 days of incurring the expenses by providing all the supporting documents.
