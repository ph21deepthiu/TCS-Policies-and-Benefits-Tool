    I. EPF(Employeesâ€™ Provident Fund)

    Employee Contribution:

    Mandatory 12% of basic salary every month.

    TCS Contribution:

    12% of basic salary every month after reducing Employee Pension Contribution.

    Benefits while in Service:

    Interest can be earned on accumulated PF balance. Partial withdrawal can be availed for stipulated reasons.

    Reasons for which you can withdraw PF:

    While in Service:

    - Purchase of house or flat
    - Build a house
    - Higher education of Children
    - Acquisition of site
    - Natural calamities
    - Permanent Total Disablement
    - Alterations/Additions of dwelling
    - Illness of family member
    - Marriage of self/dependents
    - 1 year prior to retirement
    - Repayment of Housing Loan

    Post Separation:

    - Purchase of equipment for a physically handicapped family member
    - Marriage of Self(Women only)
    - On retirement
    - Migration to permanent settlement abroad
    - Unemployment for 1 month(Partial PF withdrawal = 75%)
    - Unemployment for 2 months

    UAN(Universal Identification Number):

    UAN is allotted to every salaried employee contributing to his/her EPF account. This number remains the same irrespective of the number of jobs an employee changes.

    For UAN registration it is mandatory to submit an Aadhaar number linked to a bank account and an active mobile number. One can activate UAN by visiting the EPFO portal.

    UAN number is also available on the employee's Salary slip.


    II. Gratuity

    Eligibility Criteria:

    An employee becomes eligible for gratuity benefit after completion of service for 4 years and 8 months at TCS.

    If an employee leaves the organization earlier than 4 years and 8 months, then in the new organization, he/she must serve for another 4 years and 8 months to be eligible for the claim on Gratuity.


    III. Superannuation

    Eligibility Criteria:

    Eligible for all the employees at Grade C3 B and above.

    TCS contribution:

    15% of the monthly basic salary.


After Life Benefits



    Eligibility Criteria:

    Can be claimed by the nominee of the associate in case of unfortunate demise of the associate.

    Nominees(single or multiple) must be updated in the Global - ESS portal who will be eligible for the benefits.

    Assured Amount:

    23 lakhs or 6 times the employee's annual compensation, whichever is higher and will be equally divided among the nominees.

    Application Criteria:

    Company might connect with the family and get the necessary documents based upon the corporate requirement, to be submitted to the Government before providing the Gratuity amount to the nominees.


TATA Trust



    Purpose:

    To provide financial assistance to the associates for medical treatment or the higher education of their children.

    Membership Criteria:

    Membership fee of Rs. 250/FY deducted from the employee's salary upon enrolling.

    Eligibility Criteria:

    An associate will be eligible to claim the amount from TATA Trust, only after 1 year of continuous service at TCS.

    Benefits:

    - The financial assistance provided is in the form of an interest-free loan. For the education of children, up to 36 lakhs(18 lakh/child) is provided to the associate.

    - Loan amount will be recovered by automatic deduction from the employee's salary.


    Note:

    Associates must add nominees which could be a Spouse or Parents or both who will be eligible for the social security benefits, in the Global-ESS portal.

              

    For newly married, within 90 days one can add a beneficiary as a Spouse.
